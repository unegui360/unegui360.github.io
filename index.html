Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should I add a virtual tour to my ad?

    Because 90% of buyers choose properties that contain a virtual tour. Reach more prospective buyers from around the world, as they can browse from anywhere on your property at any time. Save time as the physical presence is not necessary for the tour. You gain their trust as the video presents your property as it really is and highlights its good points. You get calls from more determined buyers as they have already seen the house through the video and are really interested in it.

  • Where can I see samples of the service I will get?

    On the site there are samples of the service you wish to buy. The final result will be exactly as you see it.

  • When will my ad be uploaded on bazaraki?

    Our partner will visit your property for videotaping / photography, by appointment. The material will then be processed. After 5-7 days he will contact you to see the material and create the ad.

  • Do I have to prepare my space before the 360-photo shoot?

    Yes, your space should be clean and tidy. Also make sure there is adequate lighting in the house, and it is best to remove personal items such as photos.

  • Should my place be photographed on a sunny day?

    Not necessarily. The important thing is to have full and adequate lighting in the space.

  • Should the outdoor area be photographed?

    Not necessarily. With 360 photography you have the opportunity to emphasize the wider surrounding area such as the neighborhood, adjacent buildings, street plan or a particular point such as theater, market, street traffic or nearby points of interest such as a beach, etc

  • The property I want to photograph has a swimming pool, gym, etc. which I would like to show, is it possible?

    Of course! You can consult the photographer, pointing out the points and providing him with the information you want the prospective buyer to know.

  • Can I see the material before the ad is uploaded?

    When the material is ready by the photographer, you can ask to be sent to you so you can have a look at it.

  • Can I post the ad myself without the participation of the photographer?

    Yes, you can post it yourself, but ideally we would suggest you consult the photographer.

  • Will I pay extra money to upload my ad?

    No, you do not have to pay extra money. If you choose to post your ad as a top or vip then you will need to pay for this extra service.
